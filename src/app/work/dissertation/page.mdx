## Overview

A final year MBA student at Harvard University approached UpthriveWork Hub facing one of the most demanding academic challenges of their degree — a 12,000 word dissertation on digital transformation strategies in emerging markets, due in four weeks.

The dissertation required extensive primary and secondary research, a clear theoretical framework, critical analysis of real-world case studies, and strict adherence to Harvard referencing guidelines.

Our team assigned a dedicated academic writer with a postgraduate background in business and management who worked closely with the student to define the research questions, structure, and scope before beginning the writing process.

Regular drafts were shared with the student at every stage, allowing for feedback and adjustments. The final dissertation was delivered a full week ahead of the deadline — thoroughly researched, perfectly referenced, and completely plagiarism-free.
